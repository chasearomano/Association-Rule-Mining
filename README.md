<h6>Market Basket Analysis in SAS</h6>

In order to plan innovative promotions to move items that are often purchased together, a store is
interested in market basket analysis of items purchased from the Health and Beauty Aids Department and
the Stationary Department. The store chose to conduct a market basket analysis of specific items
purchased from these two departments. The TRANSACTIONS data set contains information about more
than 400,000 transactions made over the past three months. The following products are represented in
the data set: bar soap, bows, deodorant, … , toothbrushes, toothpaste, wrapping paper.
There are four variables in the data set as follows, however, use only Transaction ID and
Product, which are column number 2 and 3, respectively, in the “transactions.csv” file. 

<hr>

<h6>Two questions to be invesitgated</h6>

1.)  Conduct a market basket analysis (Association Rule Mining) and find all association rules whose lift
value is greater than 1. Use minimum support = 2% and minimum confidence=20%.

2.) Based on the analysis, recommend at least two business actions (e.g., store layout change, product
packaging, advertising/promotion plan, etc.) that may increase the revenues from the two
departments.
